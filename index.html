Haomin (Harmin) Qi (Chee)

I am a master student at UC San Diego, advised by Prof. Jingbo Shang. Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I was advised by Prof. Soung Chang Liew and Prof. Rosanna Yuen-Yan Chan. Prior to that, I worked with Prof. Min-Te (Peter) Sun at National Central University and Prof. Jun Wei Hsieh at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, Taiwan.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Software Engineering. I focus on improving the code generation capabilities of large language models (LLMs) and agentic AI systems; leveraging LLMs for program verification, repair, and quality evaluation; and enabling efficient inference and training for LLMs. My goal is to develop advanced autonomous agents with efficient reasoning that can reliably generate and maintain code at scale.

I am currently seeking summer internship opportunities in 2026.
